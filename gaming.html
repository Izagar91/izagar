Final Fantasy Announces New Expansion: Dawntrail

Hit game, Final Fantasy XIV, the critically acclaimed MMORPG has recently released news on a new expansion to it's story titled Dawntrail. Following the events of Endwalker, the Warrior of Light now seeks to go to the western content and explore new lands.

Dawntrailer will be releasing in Summer of 2024 and will include two new classes for players to play: the Melee DPS class, Viper, along with the Magical DPS, Pictographer.
